What's Covered

Home coverage that protects everything under your roof.

A good homeowners policy does more than protect your house — it protects your financial life when the unexpected hits.

Dwelling Coverage

Pays to repair or rebuild the structure of your home after a covered loss — fire, wind, hail, and more.

Personal Property

Covers your furniture, electronics, clothing, and belongings if they're stolen or damaged by a covered event.

Liability Protection

If someone is injured on your property and sues, liability coverage handles legal fees and damages — up to your limit.

Loss of Use

Pays for hotel, meals, and living expenses while your home is being repaired after a covered loss.

Other Structures

Covers detached garages, fences, sheds, and other structures on your property — not just the main house.

Medical Payments

Covers minor medical bills for guests injured on your property, regardless of fault — without a lawsuit.
