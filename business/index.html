Coverage Types

Every business faces risk. The right policy manages it.

Whether you're a solo contractor or a growing company, we build a coverage package around what your business actually needs.

General Liability

Covers bodily injury, property damage, and personal injury claims against your business. The foundation of any business policy.

Commercial Property

Protects your building, equipment, inventory, and business assets from fire, theft, vandalism, and covered weather events.

Business Interruption

Replaces lost income and covers operating expenses when a covered event forces your business to temporarily close.

Commercial Auto

Covers vehicles used for business purposes — owned, leased, or employee-owned — that a personal policy won't touch.

Workers' Compensation

Required in most states if you have employees. Covers medical costs and lost wages when a worker is injured on the job.

Professional Liability

Protects service-based businesses from claims of negligence, errors, or omissions in the work you deliver.
