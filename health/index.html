Marketplace Plan Tiers

Four tiers. One right fit for your household.

Each metal tier balances your monthly premium against what you pay when you actually use care. We help you find which balance works for your health needs and your budget.

Bronze

Lowest monthly premium. Highest out-of-pocket costs when you use care. Best for healthy individuals who rarely need medical services.

Silver

Middle ground on premiums and costs. Silver plans also unlock cost-sharing reductions for eligible households — the only tier that does.

Gold

Higher monthly premium, lower costs at the point of care. A strong fit if you have regular prescriptions, doctor visits, or ongoing treatment.

Platinum

Highest premium, lowest out-of-pocket burden. Built for people with frequent or significant medical needs who want maximum cost predictability.

Premium Tax Credit

You may qualify for help paying your premium — and not even know it.

The Premium Tax Credit is a federal subsidy that lowers your monthly health insurance cost for eligible households buying through the Marketplace.

Lower your monthly premium now

If eligible, the credit applies directly to your premium — reducing what you pay each month rather than waiting for tax season.

Based on income and household size

Eligibility is tied to your household income relative to the federal poverty level. Your advisor will check your eligibility in minutes.

Report income changes

Significant income or household size changes can affect your credit. We help you stay updated to avoid surprises at tax time.
