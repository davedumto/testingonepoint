Coverage Types

Eight types of life insurance. One that's right for you.

Each policy type serves a different purpose. Your advisor helps you understand which one fits your family's needs — not just the one that's easiest to sell.

Term Life

Affordable protection for 10, 20, or 30 years. The most cost-effective way to cover income replacement, mortgages, and growing families.

Whole Life

Lifetime coverage with fixed premiums and a guaranteed cash value component that builds over time.

Universal Life

Flexible premiums and death benefits with potential for cash value growth. Adjustable as your needs change.

Indexed Universal Life

Cash value tied to a market index — offering growth potential with downside protection built in.

Final Expense

Simplified underwriting and affordable premiums designed to cover funeral costs, burial, and end-of-life expenses.

Long-Term Care

Supports extended care needs — assisted living, nursing home, or in-home care — as part of broader retirement planning.

Group Life

Employer-sponsored group coverage that gives teams a foundational benefit at little or no cost to employees.

Critical Illness

Lump-sum payment after a diagnosis of cancer, stroke, heart attack, or other covered events — when bills hit fast.
