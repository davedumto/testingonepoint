What's Covered

Auto coverage that protects you in the moments that matter.

From fender benders to total losses, the right policy makes sure an accident doesn't derail your finances.

Liability Coverage

Covers injuries and property damage you cause to others in an accident. Required in most states — and critical if you're ever sued.

Collision Coverage

Pays to repair or replace your vehicle after a crash, regardless of who's at fault. Essential if you're financing or leasing.

Comprehensive Coverage

Covers theft, weather damage, fire, and non-collision events. One of the most overlooked but valuable add-ons.

Medical Payments

Helps cover medical bills for you and your passengers after an accident — no matter who caused it.

Uninsured Motorist

Protects you when the at-fault driver has little or no coverage. More common than you'd expect.

Roadside & Rental

Towing, flat tires, lockouts, and a rental car while yours is in the shop — the extras that make a real difference.
